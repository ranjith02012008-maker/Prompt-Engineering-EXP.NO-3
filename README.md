EX.NO:3 Exploring the different types of prompts- Straightforward Prompts - Tabular Format Prompting - Missing Word Prompting - Preceding Question Prompting.

Aim: To write the prompts for the following prompt types for 2 different scenarios of Each type.

Straightforward Prompts

Prompt:
Define photosynthesis in one sentence.

Response:
Photosynthesis is the process by which green plants use sunlight, carbon dioxide, and water to produce food (glucose) and release oxygen.

Prompt:
List three advantages of electric vehicles.

Response:
Three advantages of electric vehicles are:

They produce zero tailpipe emissions, reducing air pollution.

They have lower operating and maintenance costs compared to petrol or diesel vehicles.

They are energy-efficient and can use renewable electricity sources.

Tabular Format Prompting

Prompt:
Compare and contrast AC and DC current in a table.

Response:

Feature	AC (Alternating Current)	DC (Direct Current)
Direction of flow	Changes direction periodically	Flows in one direction only
Magnitude	Varies with time	Constant or nearly constant
Frequency	Has a frequency (e.g., 50 Hz or 60 Hz)	Zero frequency
Source	Power stations, generators	Batteries, cells, solar panels
Transmission	Efficient for long-distance transmission	Less suitable for long-distance transmission
Voltage transformation	Easily stepped up or down using transformers	Requires electronic converters
Examples of use	Household power supply	Electronic devices, electric vehicles

Preceding Question Prompting

Prompt:
Why is climate change a global concern? Explain how greenhouse gases contribute to global warming.

Response:
Climate change is a global concern because it affects all countries through rising temperatures, extreme weather events, sea-level rise, food and water insecurity, health risks, and economic instability, regardless of where the emissions originate.

Greenhouse gases such as carbon dioxide (CO₂), methane (CH₄), and nitrous oxide (N₂O) contribute to global warming by trapping heat in the Earth’s atmosphere. They allow sunlight to enter but absorb and re-emit infrared radiation from the Earth’s surface, preventing heat from escaping into space and thereby increasing the planet’s average temperature.

Prompt:
How do vaccines work? Describe the process of immunization in simple terms.

Response:
Vaccines work by training the body’s immune system to recognize and fight harmful germs without causing the disease. In simple terms, a vaccine contains a harmless form or piece of a virus or bacteria. When it enters the body, the immune system learns to recognize it and produces antibodies.

Later, if the real germ enters the body, the immune system remembers it and quickly destroys it, protecting the person from getting sick.

Missing Word Prompting

Prompt:
The capital of France is ____.

Response:
The capital of France is Paris.

Prompt:
In photosynthesis, plants absorb sunlight to produce ____.

Response:
In photosynthesis, plants absorb sunlight to produce glucose (food).

The results confirm that proper prompt design helps in producing clear, organized, and informative responses from AI systems.
